                            FASTA file with Diversity genes
      --d-gene-feature <gene_feature>
                            Gene feature corresponding to Diversity gene sequences in FASTA
                              (DRegion by default)
      --d-genes-from-species <species>
                            Species to take Diversity genes from it (human, mmu, lamaGlama, alpaca,
